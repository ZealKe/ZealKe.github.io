Why we?

We do one of the most crucial tasks - connect and help people technologically.

Help the IIT Madras community

Your work will directly contribute to helping everyone in the campus, be it the IPR team(A custom portal), the Elected Representatives(General Complaint System), the Sports Committee(Sports Portal), or even everyone in the college(Insti Dashboard). What better way to give back to the college than be a part of something that touches everyone's lives, every day, every moment.

Work with various teams

As a member of the Institute Web/Mob Ops team, be it a coodinator or intern, you will have to work in collaboration with various institute bodies, and deliver them portals and applications that are custom tailored to their needs.
It will be quite an exposure - of both understanding the teams and working on new platforms.

Learn something not taught in the class

We work with the trendiest and the most functional technologies. Node JS. LAMP Stack. Ruby On Rails. You won't get to learn these in the classroom.
Be it for a resume point, or as addition to your skills, or an experiment to explore, you get the firsthand working experience on demanding projects, as a part of a team of like minded people.

What, Why, How?

You must be wondering

  • Why should I apply for you?

    Impress me

    This point tends to be at the last, even though it should be placed before any other point: You’ll get paid. Paid for the work you do. Paid on an hourly basis. Paid to sit in front of your laptop, when you’ve already done that so far in your vacations, and probably were gonna do for the rest of it anyways. Isn’t that just cool, earning while sitting at home?
    During the course of the internship, we’ll also be training you and assisting you in learning a few platforms/frameworks, depending on the project you are assigned to and what you’d like to learn. LAMP Stack. Ruby on Rails. Android Studio. MEAN Stack. Platforms with a techy sounding names, that are hot in the market. Skills that you can proudly boast about in your resume. Languages that many of your friends in even CS wouldn’t know. Oh, did we mention than our past team members have been placed in awesome companies like Samsung, Qualcomm, Paytm, Barclays, J.P. Morgan and may more!
    We’ll also be awarding you certificates from the Institute Web/Mob Ops team of IIT Madras on successfully interning with us. You’ll also have learned at least one programming language, with enough skill to open further avenues. It’ll definitely be a help in getting you a good internship/placement, so why not put your summer to a better use?

  • Is it difficult to get selected?

    I've never been an expert

    One of the few advantages of having low awareness amongst people is that if you apply, you have a good chance of getting selected, because not a lot of people are even aware we're hiring. No, it's not tough. The work does not involve you running around, meeting people, organizing events, etc. What we do instead is just sit - sit and think. Think and code(How we spend the next three hours debugging our genius code is another thing).
    The application ought to reflect that, so it will require you to solve a few programming tasks

  • What would we be doing during the internship?

    Just for curiousity

    You'll be assisting us with certain projects that we have recieved from teams all over the institute, throughout the course of the summer. Don't worry, we'll assing you work in small chunks, and guide you through all of it. Afterall we've been there too.
    Alongwith the work, we'll also be teaching you new platforms/languages from scratch. If you put in a decent amount of effort, you'll be skilled enough with at least one boastable skill in terms of web/mobile development.
    [There might even be a treat once you all come to insti in August, in the end. :P ]

  • Lastly, how do I apply?

    I hope the app is not too long

    Although we sent out a mail for call for interns, if you missed it and are too lazy to go search your inbox, no problem. Fill this Google Form first. Also, Click here to download the application for the internship. Have a look at the app, and then read further.
    Had a look? Of course you didn't. Fine, here's what we wanted to convey: The app is quite lengthy, as a whole, but YOU DO NOT HAVE TO ATTEMPT ALL OF IT. Firstly, you are expected to do only one - WebOps or MobOps. In WebOps as well, it's not compulsory to attempt all. As it it mentioned, ATTEMPT ALL YOU CAN. NO ONE WILL BE ABLE TO DO THE WHOLE THING. We'll grade you on the basis of what you do. We need creative thinkers, not diligent noobs.
