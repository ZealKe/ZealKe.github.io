About Us


Zeal is an I.T company that started way back in 2016 November but we did not give it a name back then for it was an idea to work as a group of two to solve problems through technology.

We have however grown to a team of five who oversee and work on various projects and are hoping to grow bigger as time progresses.

The dream is to provide a platform for creativity and innovativeness in the world. It has been tremendous and we look forward to growing bigger and bigger and getting the chance to change this great nation to a Tech Zone of the Sub-Saharan Africa and Africa as a whole.

We are committed to embrace change and take chances whenever we are faced with tasks. There is no better way to do this than to walk out there and share with companies what we can do and source for opportunities to employ our skills. We let our hunger for change and the hope for a better country and world drive us to the levels we want to reach.

Our company slogan is ; Tech for change and change through Tech.
